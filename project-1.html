XPAN

This magazine is my way of saying thank you to XPAN.

Overview

Receiving the XPAN Interactive Design Bursary Award meant a lot to me. It supported me financially, but more importantly, it gave me the motivation to keep going and take my work more seriously as I grow as a designer.

While working on this project, I spent time exploring XPAN’s values and realized how much they connect with my own experience. Ideas like supporting each other, thinking bigger, staying honest, and enjoying the process are not just part of their culture — they are also shaping how I learn and create.

This magazine reflects that connection. It is both a personal project and a small way for me to show my appreciation for the opportunity XPAN has given me.
