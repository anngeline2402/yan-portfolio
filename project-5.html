Moonspirit

A magazine celebrating Vietnam’s drag queen scene.

Overview

Moonspirit is an editorial magazine spotlighting Vietnam’s drag queen community — its queens, kings, and icons. Volume 1 explores “integration without assimilation” and the artists becoming ambassadors of the LGBTQ+ community.

The issue pairs bold fashion photography with elegant editorial layout to celebrate identity, artistry, and self-expression.
