Mazonkiki

A bohemian lifestyle fashion brand blending Mediterranean warmth and feminine freedom.

Overview

Mazonkiki is a lifestyle fashion brand inspired by art, travel, nature, and feminine freedom. The brand blends bohemian elegance with Mediterranean warmth and Asian spiritual influences, creating clothing that feels comfortable, effortless, and timeless.

Centered on wellness, self-care, and harmony with nature, the identity pairs an elegant serif wordmark with a lotus mark and a warm, earthy palette of terracotta, clay, and cream.
