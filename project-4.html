Hag House

Overview

Hag House is a fantasy, witch-themed craft beer brand built for adults who want a magical drinking experience — “Magic in your Mug.”

The brand guideline covers the brand story, target audience, moodboard, logo development, colour palette, typography, and pattern — all wrapped in an earthy world of deep green, cream, and purple inspired by medieval fantasy.
