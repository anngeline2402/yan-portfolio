Snack Grad

A retro campus-dining brand — where academics meets nutrition.

Overview

Snack Grad is a retro campus-dining brand built for a SAIT student web app that centralizes nearby food options — an interactive campus map with live wait times, a star-rated dining review section, and a “Pin a Friend” feature for scheduling lunch meet-ups.

Created with Team FlowCo, the identity pairs a vintage diner mascot — a winking grad in a tasseled cap — with a bold serif wordmark, wrapped in a warm palette of red, brown, and cream with yellow-and-orange accents. My focus was the visual-identity framework and high-fidelity UI, keeping the brand consistent across the prototype and the guideline document.

👥 Team project with Paulo De Almeida, Chelsia Agaton & Amélie Perrier
