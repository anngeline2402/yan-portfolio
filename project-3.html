Snackaroo

A playful snack brand with a kangaroo mascot — “A Head Hop Above.”

Overview

Snackaroo is a fun, bold snack brand built around a cheeky kangaroo mascot and the tagline “A Head Hop Above.” The full brand guidelines cover the logo, mascot, colour, typography, and packaging in a playful purple-and-red world.

This was a team project created for Advanced Visual Design, where each of us shaped the brand’s personality and system together.

👥 Team project with Paulo Estevao De Almeida Bezerra & Louie Valondo Estrañero
